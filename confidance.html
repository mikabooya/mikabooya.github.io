about confidance

as part of vanderbilt's premier dance organization, confidance, i led visual branding and creative direction across performances and campaigns. as head of marketing, i designed promotional assets ranging from posters and flyers to video reels and social content. in my role as creative director, i planned and directed photoshoots, photographed our dancers, and translated movement into visual stories, crafting cohesive poster designs and marketing materials that reflected the energy and identity of each dancer and showcase.
