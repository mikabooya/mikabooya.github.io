the redesign

aufe is a vietnamese-rooted coffee brand making bold, intentional brews with 100% robusta beans. for this redesign, i wanted to create a visual identity that felt as strong and authentic as the coffee itself. the goal was to honor the brand's vietnamese heritage while building something modern, earthy, and unapologetically bold. i focused on minimal processing, maximum flavor in the design language—keeping it clean but full of character, just like the coffee.
