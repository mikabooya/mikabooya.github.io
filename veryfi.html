about veryfi

veryfi is a YC-backed fintech startup that turns messy receipts and documents into clean, structured data using AI. think of it as the smartest OCR on the planet automating expense management, fraud detection, and data extraction for enterprises who are tired of manual entry.

as the company scaled, they needed marketing that could translate complex AI tech into stories people actually wanted to read. that's where i came in.

the challenge

veryfi's tech is genuinely impressive: multimodal AI, real time fraud prevention, enterprise scale document processing. but explaining it without sounding like a boring pitch? that's the challenge.

i needed to create content that developers would respect, executives would understand, and actual humans would want to read. plus, it had to work everywhere: youtube demos, case studies, blog posts, 60-second social clips. oh, and drive actual business results. no pressure.

my role

at veryfi, i wear many different hats: strategist, writer, video producer, motion designer, and occasional internal hype person. i owned everything from "what should we make?" to "here's the final edit, shipping now."

my approach? data first, human second. i'd dig into analytics to figure out what people actually cared about, then translate technical lingoes into stories that didn't make people's eyes glaze over. every video, case study, and blog post was designed to move someone through the funnel, whether that meant booking a demo or sharing it with their team.
