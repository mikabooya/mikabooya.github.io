about_mika.txt

hello, i'm mika ◡̈ born and raised in ho chi minh city, vietnam, graduated from vanderbilt university, and somehow found my way to sunny bay area california.

i'm a product & content marketer with an engineering brain and a designer heart (a self taught one, actually). i learned design by experimenting, remixing, and teaching myself everything from motion to layout to storytelling. i love building stories that *look good, feel good,* and actually make sense! whether that's a campaign, a product demo, or a brand moment that people remember.

my background in engineering science taught me how to think in systems; marketing taught me how to make people care. now i get to mix both, using creative tools like premiere pro, spline, and after effects to turn complex tech into stories that click.

this portfolio is a mix of my two tracks: marketing (positioning, launches, campaigns) and creative (motion, editing, 3d, storytelling). it's a little all over the place (like myself tehee).
