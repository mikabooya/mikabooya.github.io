marketing & creative @sf

i'm a product & content marketer with an engineering brain and a a deep passion for design. i self-taught myself design by experimenting, remixing, and teaching myself everything from motion to layout to storytelling. i love building stories that *look good, feel good,* and actually make sense! whether that's a campaign, a product demo, or a brand moment that people remember.

my background in engineering science and computational biology taught me how to learn and think fast; marketing taught me how to make people care. now i get to mix both, using creative tools like premiere pro, spline, and after effects to turn complex tech into compelling stories.

this portfolio is a mix of my two tracks: marketing (positioning, launches, campaigns) and creative (motion, editing, 3d, storytelling). it's a little all over the place (like myself lol).
